Chapter 69
Ceramic products

Notes.

1

This Chapter applies only to ceramic products which
have been fired after shaping. Headings 69,04 to 69.14
apply only to such products other than those classifiable
in headings 69.01 to 69.03.

This Chapter does not cover:

(a) Products of heading 28.44;

(b) Articles of heading 68.04;

(c) Articles of Chapter 71 (for example, imitation
jewellery);

(d) Cermets of heading 81.13;

(e) Articles of Chapter 82;

(f) Electrical insulators (heading 85.48) or fittings of
insulating material of heading 85.47;

(a) Artificial teeth (heading 90.21); :

(h) Atticles of Chapter 91 (for example, clocks and
clock cases);

(i) Articles of Chapter 94 (for example, furniture, lamps
and lighting fitings, prefabricated buildings);

(k) Articles of Chapter 95 (for example, toys, games
and sports requisites);

() Articles of heading 98.06 (for example, buttons) or
