Section IV
Prepared foodstuffs; beverages, spirits and vinegar;
tobacco and manufactured tobacco substitutes

Note.

1.- In this Section the term "pellets" means products which
have been agglomerated either directly by compression
or by the addition of a binder in a proportion not
exceeding 3 % by weight
