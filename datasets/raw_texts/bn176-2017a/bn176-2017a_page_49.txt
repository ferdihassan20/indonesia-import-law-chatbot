Subheading Notes.
1.- Within any one heading of this Chapter, derivatives of a

chemical compound (or group of chemical compounds)
are 10 be classified in the same subheading as that
compound (or group of compounds) provided that they
are not more specifically covered by any other
Subheading and that there is no residual subheading
named "Other" in the series of subheadings concemed.

2.- Note 3 to Chapter 29 does not apply to the subheadings

of this Chapter.
