For the purposes of heading 30.02, the expression
‘immunological products” applies to peptides and
proteins (ather than goods of heading 29.37) which are
directly involved in the regulation of immunological
processes, such as monoclonal antibodies (MAB),
antibody fragments, antibody conjugates and antibody
fragment conjugates, interleukins, interferons (IFN),
chemokines and certain tumor necrosis factors (TNF),
growth factors (GF), hematopoietins and colony
stimulating factors (CSF).
